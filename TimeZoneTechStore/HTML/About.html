What is Time Zone Tech?
Time Zone Tech is New Zealand's finest tech store with over 5 products that can be delivered to anywhere in NZ.
How did Time Zone Tech start?
In 2003, Zark Muckerberg started selling his old stuff including video games, game consoles, computers, etc. He realised that if he could keep this up, he could become rich. So he started Time Zone Tech with his friend, Beff Jezos, in 2011.
